I won Best Use of Google APIs at Hack NYU 2020!

I teamed up with fellow NOC-mates to build BagX, a mobile application that facilitates and tracks the borrowing, returning and donating of reusable shopping bags among the NYU student community. We built the app with React Native and Google Firebase.

I won Best Newbie Hack at Hack@CEWIT 2020!

I spent a weekend away at Stony Brook University with a bunch of NOC-mates to attend Hack@CEWIT. We built Agora, a web platform that creates powerful conversations between individuals, wherever they are in the world. Individuals can anonymously submit questions they have, and the platform links these questions with members on the platform most qualified to respond to them.
